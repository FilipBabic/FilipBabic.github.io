Olympic games basketball tournament 2024

Experience the excitement of an Olympic basketball tournament with this interactive JavaScript simulation. With just a single click, you can simulate the entire tournament from start to finish, including the group stage, knockout rounds, and final medal awards.


The outcomes of each game are calculated based on a combination of the team’s rank, current form, and a random factor, making the results both strategic and unpredictable. Teams start with a performance rating derived from previous games, and their form increases or decreases depending on whether they win or lose each match.


At the end of the competition, the top three teams are awarded gold, silver, and bronze medals based on their final standings.
