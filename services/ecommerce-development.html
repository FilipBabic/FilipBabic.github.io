E-commerce development

Definition ⇓⇓⇓

E-commerce development focuses on building online stores that facilitate buying and selling of products or services over the internet. It includes features like product listings, shopping carts, payment gateways, and order management.


Understanding ⇓⇓⇓

It involves integrating features like product catalogs, shopping carts, secure payment gateways, and order management systems. E-commerce sites are designed to provide a seamless shopping experience, from browsing products to completing transactions, while ensuring security and scalability. This involves careful consideration of user interface design, backend functionality, and performance optimization to handle high volumes of transactions and support business growth.


Examples ⇓⇓⇓

Creating an e-commerce website for a retail business to sell clothing, setting up an online marketplace for handmade crafts or developing a subscription-based service platform.
